# 📊 Supply Chain & Inventory Optimization Dashboard

## 🚀 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing supply chain operations using **Python and Power BI**.
It involves cleaning messy real-world-like data, building interactive dashboards, and generating business insights to improve inventory management and supplier performance.

The objective is to:

* Reduce stockouts
* Improve inventory efficiency
* Analyze supplier performance
* Support data-driven decision making

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Python** (Pandas, NumPy) – Data Cleaning & Processing
* **SQL** – Messy Data Generation
* **Power BI** – Dashboard & Visualization

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Generated **50,000+ rows of messy supply chain data**
* Includes:

  * Order details
  * Product & category
  * Warehouse & region
  * Supplier data
  * Revenue & cost
  * Delivery time

---

## 🔧 Data Cleaning (Python)

Key steps performed:

* Handled missing and null values
* Fixed inconsistent date formats
* Removed invalid entries (e.g., negative stock, text in numeric fields)
* Converted data types
* Created new calculated fields

---

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 1️⃣ Executive Overview

* Total Revenue, Profit, Profit Margin
* Inventory Turnover
* Stockout Rate
* Revenue Trend
* Regional Profit Contribution
* Category-wise Revenue

---

### 2️⃣ Inventory Optimization

* Inventory Turnover by Product
* Warehouse Stock Levels
* Stockout Count by Warehouse
* Order Quantity by Category

---

### 3️⃣ Supplier Performance

* Supplier Revenue Contribution
* Supplier Delivery Performance
* Stockouts by Supplier
* Supplier Regional Distribution

---

## 📈 Key KPIs

* Total Revenue
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Inventory Turnover
* Avg Delivery Days
* Stockout Rate

## 💡 Key Insights

* 📌 Delhi warehouse shows highest stockout incidents
* 📌 Inventory turnover is low for certain products, indicating slow movement
* 📌 Stationary category has highest demand
* 📌 Supplier delivery performance varies across regions
